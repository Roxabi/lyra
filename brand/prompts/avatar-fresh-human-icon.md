# Avatar — Lyra · Fresh Human · Icon Style

## Intent

Lyra's avatar should feel like a person you'd trust with your most important work —
not an anime character, not an abstract AI, not a celebrity. A real, intelligent,
approachable human face. Warm without being saccharine. Smart without being cold.

The Forge Orange accent light is the only brand signal. Everything else is real.

---

## Primary Prompt

Close-up portrait of a young woman, mid-to-late twenties. Natural skin, clean features,
calm and intelligent expression — confident without posturing. Slight three-quarter angle,
eyes directly engaged. Minimal styling: clean hair pulled back or loose, no heavy makeup.

Lighting: soft cinematic key light from the left (cool, slightly blue-grey), warm Forge Orange
rim light from the right (#e85d04) — like the glow of a monitor or ember, not a flash.
Background: near-black obsidian (#0a0a0f), slightly out of focus.

Icon crop: head and upper shoulders, centered, plenty of breathing room around the face.
Shallow depth of field, sharp on eyes and nose, soft fall-off on background.

Mood: the face of a tool you can trust. Present, grounded, with an undercurrent of warmth.
Photorealistic. Natural skin texture. Real catch lights in the eyes.

---

## Variant A — Day / Studio (primary candidate)

Soft diffused studio light, neutral key, Forge Orange accent rim on right edge.
No dramatic shadows. Clean obsidian background. Expression: calm attentiveness,
slight upward lift at the corners of the mouth — not smiling, but warm.

---

## Variant B — Evening / Working (secondary candidate)

Low ambient light. Forge Orange is the dominant source — warm, directional, like a screen glow.
Cooler shadows on the left side. Expression: focus. She's thinking. Eyes slightly narrowed.
Background: very dark, slightly textured — suggests an inhabited space, not a studio.

---

## Variant C — Minimal / Icon-first (app icon candidate)

Maximum simplicity. Extreme shallow DOF, background pure obsidian.
Face fills 60% of frame. No rim light distraction — single soft key light only.
Expression: direct eye contact, neutral warmth. Works at 64px without losing readability.

---

## Brand Alignment Notes

- Forge Orange (#e85d04) must appear as a light accent, not a filter or color grade
- Obsidian (#0a0a0f) background — never pure black, never grey
- No jewelry, no costume, no props — the face is the entire statement
- Avoid beauty-retouching aesthetics: real pores, real skin, real eyes
- The image should pass for a real person — not a headshot of a stock model,
  more like an editorial portrait of someone who builds things
